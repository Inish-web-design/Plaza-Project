About The Plaza

Culture, Community, Connection

Our Mission

The Plaza Project exists to provide world-class cultural, recreational, and social facilities that bring people together, celebrate our heritage, and nurture the next generation of community leaders.

Cultural Designation Benefits

Musical Events

Concerts & performances

Theatre & Drama

Productions & workshops

Art & Exhibitions

Galleries & craft fairs

Our official cultural designation allows us to host virtually any indoor activity:

  • Musical performances and concerts
  • Theatre and dramatic productions
  • Art exhibitions and craft fairs
  • Educational workshops and lectures
  • Religious and spiritual gatherings
  • Social clubs and hobby groups
  • Fitness classes and wellness programs
  • Business meetings and conferences

What We Cannot Host: Outdoor sports requiring large field spaces (football, hurling, etc.) - though indoor sports and activities are welcome.
