Our Community Heritage

Our charming small hall has been the heart of community gatherings since 1942. This intimate venue, with its warm atmosphere and flexible space, perfectly accommodates a variety of events and activities.

What Makes Our Small Hall Special

Community Ownership

100% owned by Buncrana community

Fully Licensed Bar

Well-stocked social atmosphere

  • Community Ownership: This hall is owned entirely by the Buncrana community. Should it ever be sold, all proceeds are legally committed to supporting another community project, ensuring our investment always benefits local people.
  • Versatile Event Space: Perfect for intimate gatherings of 50-80 people
  • Fully Licensed Bar: Our well-stocked bar area creates a welcoming social atmosphere
  • Professional Sound System: Quality audio equipment for music and announcements
  • Accessible Facilities: Ground floor access with disabled-friendly amenities

Regular Activities

Birthday Parties

Children & adult celebrations

Old Time Dancing

Traditional dancing sessions

  • Children's Birthday Parties: Affordable packages with decorations and entertainment options
  • Adult Celebrations: Birthday parties, anniversary celebrations, retirement parties
  • Old Time Dancing: Traditional Irish and international folk dancing
  • Community Meetings: Local organizations and clubs regular gathering space
  • Private Functions: Baby showers, engagement parties, small weddings

Booking Information: Competitive rates with special discounts for community groups and regular bookings.
