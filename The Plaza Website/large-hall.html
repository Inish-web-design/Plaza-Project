Future Vision

Our large hall, with its soaring ceilings and commanding presence, awaits a complete transformation that will restore it to its former glory and beyond.

Current Status

Floor Restoration

50% of original oak floor salvageable

Architectural Planning

Future Capacity

300-400 guests when complete

  • Floor Restoration: Professional assessment revealed that 50% of the original 1958 oak sprung dance floor could be saved and restored
  • Architectural Planning: Complete renovation plans include modern amenities while preserving historical character
  • Capacity: Will accommodate 300-400 guests when renovation is complete
  • Future Features: State-of-the-art lighting and sound systems, climate control, accessible facilities, and flexible staging options

Heritage Preservation

The renovation will honor the hall's big band era heritage with period-appropriate design elements alongside modern functionality. This magnificent space will once again serve as the cultural heartbeat of our community.
