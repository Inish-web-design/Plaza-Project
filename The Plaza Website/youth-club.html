State-of-the-Art Youth Facilities

After months of careful construction and planning, our state-of-the-art youth club is nearly ready to welcome young people aged 12-25 from across Buncrana and surrounding areas.

World-Class Facilities Include

Community Café

Healthy, affordable meals & snacks

Music Room

Piano & band practice space

Gaming Hub

Latest consoles & gaming PCs

Games Zone

Pool, table tennis & board games

Mini Cinema

40-seat cinema for movie nights

Meeting Room

Quiet space for homework & study

Outdoor Area

Safe, supervised outdoor space

Planned Programs

  • After-school homework support - Dedicated quiet space and mentoring
  • Life skills workshops - Cooking, budgeting, job interview preparation
  • Creative workshops - Music production, film making, art classes
  • Sports tournaments and leagues - Organized competitions and team activities
  • Mental health and wellbeing support - Professional guidance and peer support
  • Career guidance and mentorship programs - Preparing for the future

Youth Input Welcome: We're actively seeking suggestions from young people about activities, programs, and improvements they'd like to see.
